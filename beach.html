Beach Tourism

Pristine white sands, crystal-clear waters, and stunning coastal scenery await along the shores of Zamboanga del Norte.

Zamboanga del Norte is blessed with one of the Philippines' most scenic coastlines, stretching along both the Sulu Sea and the Bohol Sea. Its beaches range from world-renowned resort destinations to secluded coves known only to locals, offering every kind of beach experience from lively water sports hubs to peaceful white-sand retreats.

The crown jewel is Dakak Beach Resort in Dapitan City, considered among the finest beach destinations in Mindanao and the entire country. Beyond Dakak, the province's islands and coastal municipalities offer beaches of extraordinary natural beauty, many still largely undiscovered by mass tourism.

Featured Beach Destinations

Explore the finest beaches and coastal escapes in Zamboanga del Norte.

World-Class Resort

Dakak Beach Resort

Arguably the most famous beach resort in Mindanao, Dakak in Dapitan City features powdery white sand, turquoise waters, and lush hillside gardens. A premier destination for families, honeymooners, and adventure seekers alike.

Dapitan City Resort Beach
Island Escape

Sibutad Island

A stunning island municipality facing the Sulu Sea, Sibutad is known for its pristine coral reefs, white sand bars, and crystal-clear waters perfect for snorkeling and swimming.

Sibutad Island Beach
Marine Sanctuary

Aliguay Island

This small island off Dapitan is surrounded by rich coral gardens and serves as a protected marine sanctuary. Ideal for snorkeling, diving, and eco-friendly island hopping.

Dapitan City Marine Park
Local Favorite

Rock Dive Inn

Rock Dive Inn Liloy offers calm, clear waters and a relaxed atmosphere. Popular for swimming, diving and waterfront dining, with fresh seafood readily available from local vendors.

Liloy Town Beach
River-Sea Meeting

Salug River Mouth Beach

A unique natural attraction where the Salug River meets the Bohol Sea, creating a scenic landscape of fresh and saltwater mixing along a broad sandy shore popular with local families.

Salug River Beach
Coastal Town

Sergio Osmeña Sr. Beach

A quiet and scenic coastal beach in the municipality named after the Philippines' fourth president. Offers calm waters, fishing boat views, and a serene escape from urban life.

Sergio Osmeña Sr. Municipal Beach

Travel Tips & Visitor Information

  • Best beach season runs from November to May when seas are calm and skies are clear.
  • Dakak Beach Resort requires advance reservations, especially during weekends and holidays.
  • Aliguay Island is reached by outrigger boat from Dapitan City — arrange with local boatmen.
  • Bring reef-safe sunscreen to protect the coral ecosystems of marine sanctuaries.
  • Sibutad Island requires a short ferry ride — check schedules at the Dipolog port.
  • Respect local fishing communities near beaches; avoid littering on the shorelines.
