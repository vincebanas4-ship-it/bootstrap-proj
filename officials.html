Notes on Provincial Leadership

  • The Province of Zamboanga del Norte was created in 1952, with Felipe B. Azcuna serving as its first Governor.
  • Felipe B. Azcuna also served as the province's first Vice Governor before his first gubernatorial term.
  • Isagani S. Amatong served two non-consecutive terms as Governor: 1986–1995 and 1998–2004.
  • Rolando E. Yebes had the longest uninterrupted single tenure as Governor, serving from 2004 to 2013.
  • Rosalina G. Jalosjos (2022–2025) was the first woman to serve as Governor of Zamboanga del Norte.
  • Darel Dexter T. Uy assumed the governorship in 2025 and is the current chief executive of the province.
  • Provincial officials serve 3-year terms with a maximum of three consecutive terms under the Local Government Code of 1991.
